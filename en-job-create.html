Creating a job on Pulsifi is quick and straightforward. Follow the steps below to get started with your job listing, from entering job details to setting up assessments.

 

Step 1: Log in to Your Pulsifi Account

  • Open the Pulsifi platform and log in using your credentials. Or click here to log in.
  • You will be directed to the Pulsifi's App.

Step 2: Click the "Create Job" Button

  • Once you are logged in, locate the "Create Job" button on the top-right corner of your screen.
  • Click the button to begin the job creation process.

Step 3: Enter Job Details

You will be taken to the Job Creation form. Here's a breakdown of the fields you'll need to fill out:

  1. Job Title: Enter the title of the job you're hiring for.
  2. Job Description: Provide a detailed description of the role, including responsibilities and expectations.
  3. Requirements: List the skills, qualifications, and experience necessary for the role.
  4. Location: Specify where the job is based, or if it’s remote.
  5. Employment Type: Choose from full-time, part-time, contract, or other employment types.

Step 4: Add Assessments (Optional)

  • After entering job details, you can enhance your hiring process by adding relevant assessments to your job listing. Pulsifi offers a variety of assessments to evaluate candidates' skills and fit.
  • Select appropriate assessments from the dropdown list, or skip this step if you prefer to add them later.

Step 5: Review and Publish

  • After filling out the job details and adding assessments (if any), review your job listing.
  • Once everything looks good, click "Publish" to make the job live. If you’re not ready yet, you can save it as a draft.

Step 6: Managing Your Job Listing

  • After publishing, you can manage your job listing from the Job Dashboard. Here, you can view applicants, edit the job posting, or close the job when a suitable candidate is found.

FAQs

1. Can I edit my job posting after it's published?
Yes! You can always go back to the Job Dashboard and make updates to your job details or assessments.

2. Can I save a job listing as a draft?
Absolutely. If you need more time, you can save the job listing as a draft and publish it when you're ready.

3. How do I add assessments?
When creating a job, you'll have the option to add assessments. Choose from the available tests in the dropdown, or consult with your HR team to determine the best assessments for the role.
